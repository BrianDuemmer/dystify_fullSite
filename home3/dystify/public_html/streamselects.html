Stream Selects

From the community, for the community. Exceptional songs for exceptional people.

Stream Selects are, as the name implies, songs chosen by the viewers of the 24/7 Nintendo Music Live Stream that have been added to the stream.

Video game Stream Selects songs do not have to be related to Nintendo at all—they can be from any system. Songs from other media such as movies or anime should have a (loose) connection to Nintendo.

Due to the non-Nintendo nature of these songs they are very hard to obtain, and also sorted into Volumes.

Each Volume has a limited amount of songs and there's always a break after a Volume has been completed. How you pick songs may vary from Volume to Volume.

Where it all began...

Volume 1 of the Stream Selects was initally met with scepticism. After some time the value of this concept had been understood and the Volume was completed quickly. Each song went for 20,000 Rupees, or could be picked as Twili reward.
