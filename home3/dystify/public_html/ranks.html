Ranks & Commands

Watch the stream ‚Äì Unlock cool rewards: Ranks on the 24/7 Nintendo Music Stream.

By watching the stream and being active in chat you gain hours and Rupees over time. At certain milestones you will be able to unlock new ranks which come with special perks. Those range from unlocking special commands, to picking your own custom title and lots of Rupee-based rewards.

There are two types of ranks, dynamic and static ranks. Dynamic ranks change based on how long you have been watching the stream, while static ranks can only be assigned manually. Everyone can have one dynamic and one static rank currently. More ranks will be added in the future.

    What's a Guardian?

    Guardians are ancient machines created by the Sheikah, originally created to aid in the battle against Calamity Ganon. Guardians are under the control of Calamity Ganon during the events of Breath of the Wild and are scattered across Hyrule, attacking those who approach.
